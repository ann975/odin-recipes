A Little Bit of Fancy

Description:

A healthy heart,
comes from satsifiying craving, not by skimping on carbs.
A few lemons,
gives it a punch and goes a long way.
Shrimp,
should be fresh not frozen, please.

You will need:

  • olive oil
    • truffle oil works magic
    • regular olive oil is necessary
  • butter
  • garlic
  • shrimp
  • salt and pepper
  • white wine or broth
  • red pepper flakes
  • lemon juice
  • parsley
    • recipe does call for parsley but personally not a fan

Steps:

Do what you need to do with boiling the pasta and heating the oil/butter

  1. Toss garlic and shrimp in pan, salt and pepper
  2. Pour in wine and red pepper flakes
  3. Stir remaining butter, lemon juice, (parsley)
