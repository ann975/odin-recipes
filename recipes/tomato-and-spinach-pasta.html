Can't Forget the Veggies

Description:

All the skinny girls always pout at their salads. Why not make it a little fun and add pasta instead. Let's show everyone what it means to have a soul and add more cheese than you orginally thought necessary.

You will need:

  • penne pasta
  • yellow onion
  • garlic
  • olive oil
  • tomatoes
  • spices
    • salt
    • pepper
    • oregano
    • red pepper
    • basil
  • tomato paste
  • cream cheese
  • parmesan
  • I almost forgot spinach

How to Make:

  1. boil pasta
  2. prepare tomato sauce, dice onions, mince garlic, add in skillet, salt and pepper, add tomato paste, yadayadaya
  3. add cream cheese and parmesan, then spinach
  4. start from 4
